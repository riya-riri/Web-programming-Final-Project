Our mission

NIJ exists solely for the purpose of becoming a comfortable and supportive community for the international students during their university years.

Our vision

Our vision for 2026-2027 academic year is:

  1. To continue support new and current international students at EUH
  2. To hold events yearly for bonding and creation of fun university life

What we do?

Since our purpose is to support international students, we:

  • Hold pre-arrival orientation sessions with the Faculty members
  • Do tours around the university and surrounding areas after arriving in Japan
  • Plan and organize welcome events and holiday-specific events for socializing
  • Facilitate cross-cultural collaborative events between PUH by assisting the Faculty members of EUH
